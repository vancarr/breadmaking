About Me:

My whole bread journey began at a house party.

The drinks were strong, my friends- the hosts- were occupied, and the apartment was crowded.

I was stuck in the kitchen and my only option for entertainment was to talk to a stranger.

A stranger who made his own bread. Daily. With his own sourdough starter.

"It's really easy. Anyone can make bread."

I took that as a challenge. A few weeks later, instead of buying a baguette the next time I planned spaghetti dinner, I looked up a bread recipe.

Then another bread recipe because no one has time to let bread rise for 8 hours. Then another because it's 7pm and no body got time at 2 hour bread recipe. I eventually settled on a 1 hour recipe.

The bread was... edible. But I knew I could do better. So I tried again.

And again.

Trying different techniques, buying more equipement, trying different recipes.

I needed somewhere to put all my notes about bread. So I picked it as the topic for my coding class personal project.

And the rest is history. Enjoy!
